Note and Usage

This is "Unofficial/Fan made Exp Calculator for DelugeRPG (up to 100M Exp) - A fork of Psyduck's Exp Calculator".

  1. Left Panel - Calculator and Right Panel - List of Wild Pokemon & Trainers Exp.

  2. Enter your Current Exp and your Target Exp. Note: It assumes you have already beaten all region gym leaders and that your pokemon is at level 100.

  3. It will show a solution button. You can battle any other trainers that give the same amount of exp as those in the suggested solution.

  4. If a solution is not automatically generated or Exp to target is not turned to 0, add some exp from the trainer list on the right by clicking on them.

  5. If you want to try other DelugeRPG Exp Calculator for DelugeRPG - visit Aihvz 100K Exp Calculator and Official Exp Calculator.

   About

Aihvz Exp Calculator for DelugeRPG is based on Psyduck Exp Calculator. It only works for level 100 pokemon and beaten all the region gym leaders.
